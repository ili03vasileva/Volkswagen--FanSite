The history began in 1937...

Wolfsburg, Lower Saxony, Germany.

Volkswagen is a German automobile manufacturer headquartered in Wolfsburg,LowerSaxony,Germany.Founded in 1937 by the German Labour Front under the Nazi Party and revived into a global brand after World WarII by the British Army officer Ivan Hirst, it is known for the iconic Beetle and serves as the flagship brand of the Volkswagen Group,the largest automotive manufacturer by worldwide sales in 2016 and 2017.The group's biggest market is in China,which delivers 40 percent of its sales and profits.Its name is derived from the German-language terms VolkandWagen, translating to "people'scar" when combined.
